1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:

Account & identity information

  • Name and email address
  • Profile photo / avatar URL (if provided by your sign-in provider)
  • Sign-in identifiers from Apple Sign In or Google Sign-In
  • Device identifiers used to maintain your session and guest experience

Baby profile information

  • Baby nickname or name you choose to save
  • Gender and date of birth (if you provide them)
  • Photos you upload for portrait generation

Generated content & usage data

  • AI-generated portrait images and related generation history
  • Template / style selections and in-app activity needed to operate features
  • App diagnostics and technical logs (for example, error events) to keep the service reliable

Purchases & subscriptions

  • Subscription and purchase status processed through Apple App Store / Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your full payment card details; those are handled by Apple or Google

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the App’s features
  • Generate AI baby portraits from photos you upload
  • Manage accounts, credits, subscriptions, and purchases
  • Respond to support requests and communicate about the App
  • Protect security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations

3. AI Data Processing

Uploaded photos are used only to generate the portraits you request. We process them securely and do not sell your photos or personal data. We do not use your photos to train our own AI models without your explicit consent.

When you request a photoshoot, your uploaded image and related prompt/style information are sent to trusted third-party AI processing providers solely to create your generated portrait. Those providers process the content to fulfill your request and are not authorized by us to use your photos to sell advertising or to train models for unrelated purposes.

Photos and generated images are stored securely in connection with your account so you can view, manage, and re-use them in the App. They remain available until you delete them in the App or until your account data is deleted (see Data Retention below). We do not apply a hidden short-term auto-delete timer that removes photos without your action; you stay in control of when photos are removed from your account.

You may delete individual photos, baby profiles, or generated results from within the App where those controls are available. You may also contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only as needed to operate the App:

  • AI processing providers — to generate portraits from your uploads
  • Cloud hosting & storage providers — to store and deliver images and app data securely
  • Authentication providers — Apple and/or Google for sign-in
  • App stores & billing platforms — Apple and Google for purchases and subscription status
  • Legal / safety — when required by law or to protect users, rights, and security

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share photos for marketing lists or third-party advertising networks.

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded photos & generated portraits: retained while associated with your account so you can access them in the App, until you delete them or request account deletion.
  • Account information: retained while your account is active and as needed for support, billing records, and legal compliance.
  • After deletion requests: we permanently remove account-linked data from our active systems, subject to limited backups or records we may need to keep for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal obligations.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of photo and account data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls, and secure cloud storage. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work continuously to protect your information.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. Photos of babies/children should only be uploaded by an adult who has the right to do so. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 for their own accounts. If you believe a child’s information was submitted improperly, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete photos, generated images, or your account data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

You can exercise many of these controls in the App (for example, deleting photos or profiles). For account deletion or other privacy requests, email us at mahesh@yudiz.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and as required by applicable law.

If you use Sign in with Apple or Google, you can also manage linked permissions through those providers’ account settings.
